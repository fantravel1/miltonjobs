About Restaurant & Food Service Jobs in Milton

Milton's restaurant scene is thriving with diverse cuisine options. From family restaurants on Main Street to fast food chains, bubble tea shops, and cafes, there are numerous food service positions available. Roles include line cooks, prep cooks, servers, hosts, baristas, dishwashers, and restaurant managers.

Key Skills & Requirements

  • Food Handler Certification (required)
  • Smart Serve certification for serving alcohol
  • Ability to work evenings and weekends
  • Fast-paced work environment
  • Customer service skills
  • Teamwork and communication
