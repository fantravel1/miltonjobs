About Healthcare Jobs in Milton

Healthcare is one of Milton's fastest-growing sectors. Milton District Hospital, walk-in clinics, dental offices, physiotherapy clinics, and long-term care homes all have ongoing staffing needs. Roles range from personal support workers (PSWs) and registered nurses to medical office administrators, dental hygienists, and physicians.

Key Skills & Requirements

  • CPR/First Aid certification
  • Strong communication skills

  • Ability to work rotating shifts
  • Ontario healthcare registration (if applicable)
