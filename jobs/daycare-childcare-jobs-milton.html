About Daycare & Childcare Jobs in Milton

Milton's young, growing population creates strong demand for childcare professionals. Registered Early Childhood Educators (RECEs), daycare assistants, and before/after school program staff are needed at licensed childcare centres, home daycares, and school-based programs across the town.

  • Strong communication with parents
